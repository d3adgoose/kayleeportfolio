Kaylee Linette Morales

M.S. Computer Science (Game Development) @ USC

I'm a producer/game designer dedicated to accessibility, representation, and cognitive inclusion. I’m currently pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science with a focus on Game Development at the University of Southern California, where I am a recipient of the Gerald A. Lawson USC Games Fund Supported by PlayStation Career Paths.

I earned my B.S. in Computer Science: Game Design from the University of California, Santa Cruz. Drawing from experience in product management, Scrum, software development, and UI/UX. I build experiences that resonate widely and invite more players to feel seen in play.
